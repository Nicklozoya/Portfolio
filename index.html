From in front of a camera, to in front of a computer.

My journey started as a photographer. Where I applied my creative skills to craft beautiful images. While I loved it, and still do. I wanted to challenge myself with something else. Then I discovered coding. My intrinsic creative ability, coupled with my drive, and persistance aligned perfectly with tech. I fell in love with it. I started out self studying just to get a feel about how coding is, But I felt like I needed more structure, and wanted to connect with more likeminded individuals. I decided to join a local bootcamp near me, and was immersered in an extensive full-time bootcamp hosted by DigitalCrafts. Within this bootcamp I learned industry demanding technologies such as React, Postgres, Express.js and many more. I also worked in teams that used agile methods, which required version control, and the ability to work well with others. I'm fully equipped and ready to translate my skills into a proffessional setting. I can't wait to see what the future holds.
